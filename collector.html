INFO:

Avoid the Boxes and collect as many Hearts as you can before getting crushed! Every 20 points, a new life is earned! Beware, every 30 seconds, the Boxes seem to fall faster and faster... Powerups are dropping from the sky as well, be sure to gather those up!

FEATURES:

  • Costume Shop
    • -Customize your box!
  • Gamepad support

CONTROLS:

  • Keyboard
    • -AD/Arrow Keys - Move Left/Right
    -Mouse - Navigate Menu
    -W/Space/Up Arrow - Jump
    -Escape - Pause
  • Xbox 360/Xbox One Controller
    • -Left Analog/Directional Pad - Move Left/Right / Navigate Menu
    -A - Jump
    -Start - Pause
