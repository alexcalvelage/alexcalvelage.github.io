DESCRIPTION:

A cheese cube inspired challenge map based on a fishtank. Find all of the portraits and escape!

V1.4.3

Features

  • Timed Barriers
  • Timed Gameplay
  • MWR Weapons
  • Perk Shrine
    • -Gives random perk upon purchase
  • Multiple Gamemodes
    • -Classic
    --How it was designed!
    -Chemophobia
    --Toxic water doesn't rise
    -Stranded
    --Toxic water doesn't rise, you can't leave the starting room!
    -Snooze
    --Barriers are no longer timed
  • Optional Gun Game supported in all modes
  • Various secret Easter Eggs
  • Buyable Ending

    • BO3 Perks + Reaper Perks

    • Quick Revive
    • Mule Kick
    • Stamin-Up
    • Juggernog
    • Speed Cola
    • Deadshot Daiquiri
    • Bandolier Bandit
    • Winter's Wail
    • PHD Slider
    • Dr. Double
